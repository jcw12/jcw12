Welcome!

I'm a geospatial scientist, passionate about using geospatial solutions to solve problems.

Feel free to look below at some of my skills, experience and past projects.

Projects

I have a broad range of experience, from working as a Geospatial Consultant at Spatial Vision, a Research Assistant with the RMIT Remote Sensing Lab, and as a Spatial Research Intern at the Australian Urban Observatory.

Post-Disaster Damage Assessment using Machine Learning & SAR.

My capstone project involved building a globally transferrable machine learning model that could map damage to buildings using Sentinel-1 Synthetic Aperture Radar.

Sharing Open Data from the NSW Natural Resources Commission.

I was responsible for visualising, commmunicating, managing & publishing the spatial data outputs of the NRC on the NSW Spatial Collaboration Portal, an Esri Enterprise / Portal environment, and the Terrestrial Ecosystem Research Network (TERN).

High resolution mapping of the 2009 South Australian Heat Wave.

Using Python and XML to automate interpolation in ArcGIS Pro, I utilised data from weather stations to produce high resolution daily maps of heat from the 2009 heatwave.

Environmental Economic Accounting Dashboard

Working as part of a large team at Spatial Vision, I assisted with data visualisation using Geoserver & QGIS for the award-winning EEA Dashboard.
